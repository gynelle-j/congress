**Location:**

**Shangri-La, Singapore**

**Features:**

Plenary:

8 Booths

28 Microphones

500 Receivers

10 Breakouts, 12 Microphones in each area

**Date:**

**Aug 08**

**Notes:**

High Profile event, Prime Minister of Singapore opened the event.

Additional Monitors were provided to Interpreters in each booth.
